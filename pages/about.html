About SoftTech Institute

Empowering the next generation of software developers with world-class training and industry-ready skills since 2021.

Our Story

SoftTech Institute was founded in 2021 with a simple yet powerful vision: to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry requirements. Our founders, experienced software engineers and educators, recognized the need for practical, hands-on training that prepares students for real-world challenges.

Since our inception, we have trained over 500 students, with 95% of our graduates successfully transitioning into rewarding tech careers. Our success is built on a foundation of excellence, innovation, and unwavering commitment to student success.
